ECE Emerge SQ 2026
ECE Emerge bridges critical knowledge gaps for first-year electrical and computer engineering students, providing essential context for future studies while developing practical skills for future labs. You'll explore key electrical and computer engineering concepts through design activities involving circuits, signals, and communication systems. By applying MATLAB and completing hands-on lab exercises, you'll work collaboratively to build a functional device that demonstrates these concepts, strengthening your technical foundation for continued engineering education.

Communication

Important announcements will be made on Canvas or during class. You are expected to attend class regularly and to monitor Canvas for updates.
There is a Discord channel dedicated to this course where you can discuss course materials with your peers and contact the TAs outside of lectures, labs, and office hours.

Join the course Discord server using the following link: https://discord.gg/3NWh8UvjQG

After joining, please read the instructions in the #onboarding channel to learn how to use the server. You may post questions related to lecture notes, labs, and assignments.

For administrative or confidential matters, use the Canvas email feature rather than emailing the instructor directly. This helps ensure a timely response.
