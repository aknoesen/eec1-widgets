Module 10: Team Project

Module 10 is the final week of ECE Emerge. Lab sessions are dedicated to completing and polishing the capstone scale. The course closes with project demonstrations and the final exam.

This module covers:
* Final integration, calibration, and performance verification of the scale
* Transition from breadboard prototype to soldered final board
* Project demonstration and technical report delivery
* Final exam covering all course concepts

Project Description: ECE Emerge Project

Lab Sessions

Lab time this week is focused on completing the capstone scale to demonstration-ready condition.

  • Final Calibration: Verify that the scale maps the full 0 to 2 kg range accurately and can resolve a single jelly bean.
  • Soldered Board: Transition the verified breadboard circuit to the soldered final board and confirm performance is maintained.
  • Demonstration: Present your working scale to the teaching team, showing detection of a known weight.

Final Exam

The final exam covers the full course: circuits, signals, energy storage, filters, op-amps, and instrumentation. Review your module assessments and lab reports as preparation.
