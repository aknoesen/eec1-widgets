Module 9: Team Project

Module 9 is a dedicated project work week. There is no lecture this week (Memorial Day holiday). Your team uses the lab sessions to continue building, testing, and refining the capstone scale.

This module covers:
* Continued breadboard bring-up and debugging of the full scale signal chain
* Integration of hardware and software components
* Team coordination and milestone tracking toward the final demonstration

Project Description: ECE Emerge Project

Lab Sessions

This week's lab time is unstructured project work. Your team sets the agenda based on your current bring-up status and the remaining milestones in your Design Document.

  • Hardware Debugging: Diagnose and resolve any discrepancies found during the Module 8 bring-up.
  • Software Integration: Connect your MATLAB or Python interface to the hardware and verify the calibration routine.
  • Milestone Review: Assess your team's progress against the project milestones and adjust your plan for the final week.
